Events

At CMR Engineering College, events are an integral part of campus life. From technical workshops, hackathons, and seminars to cultural fests, sports meets, and alumni gatherings, our calendar is always vibrant. These events provide students with opportunities to showcase talent, build teamwork, and gain industry exposure. Each occasion reflects our commitment to holistic development, ensuring that learning goes beyond classrooms and labs.


Technical Events:Our college regularly hosts workshops, hackathons, coding competitions, and guest lectures by industry experts. These events help students gain hands-on experience, sharpen problem-solving skills, and stay updated with the latest technologies.


Cultural Events:Annual fests, music nights, dance competitions, and drama performances bring vibrancy to campus life. These events celebrate diversity, creativity, and teamwork, giving students a platform to showcase their talents beyond academics.
