Courses We Offer

It offers a wide range of undergraduate (B.Tech), postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA), and PhD programs across engineering, technology, and management disciplines. The most popular courses include Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Cyber Security, and MBA.

Undergraduate(B.Tech) Program

The College offers diverse undergraduate (B.Tech) courses designed to build strong foundations in engineering and technology. Popular streams include Computer Science & Engineering (CSE), Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning, Data Science, Cyber Security, Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE), Information Technology (IT), as well as core branches like Mechanical, Electrical, and Civil Engineering.

Postgraduate(M.Tech & MBA) Program

It include specializations such as Computer Science & Engineering, Embedded Systems, Cyber Security, Power Electronics, and Structural Engineering, each spanning 2 years with admissions through GATE/TS PGECET. College offers a comprehensive Master of Business Administration (MBA) program designed to develop leadership, strategic thinking, and managerial skills.

PhD program

Research programs are offered in fields such as Computer Science & Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Electrical & Electronics Engineering, and Management Studies (MBA).

Our Campus

CMR Engineering College’s campus in Hyderabad spans 10 acres with over 1,75,000 sq. ft. of built-up area, featuring modern classrooms, labs, libraries, and a state-of-the-art auditorium, all set in a peaceful environment along the Hyderabad–Nagpur highway (NH7).
